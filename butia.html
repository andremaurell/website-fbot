ABOUT THE TEAM

ButiaBots

The category consist in develop autonomous robots integrated with services and assistant technologies to perform tasks in a domestic environment. Our robot, Doris (Domestic Robotic Intelligent System), is a partnership between Universidade Federal do Rio Grande (FURG) and Universidade Federal de Santa Maria (UFSM). The robot, during the competition, has to go through a series of tasks to evaluate it's capabilities and performance in activities that requires the concepts of human-machine colaboration and interaction, recognize and manipulate objects, navigate in a dinamic environment and computer vision, all of that in a realistic and non padronized domestic place.
